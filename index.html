About Us

MBScholarships is a global platform dedicated to connecting international students with life-changing opportunities, including fully funded scholarships, internships, fellowships, and self-learning resources. We aim to eliminate financial and informational barriers that often hinder talented individuals from reaching their full academic and professional potential. By curating verified opportunities from reputable institutions and organizations worldwide, we ensure that students—regardless of their background—have equal access to quality education and career advancement.

FAQ

We are here to help you 7 days a week and respond within 24 hours. Plus, you can find most answers to your questions right on this page.

What is MBScholarships?

MBScholarships is a global platform that helps international students find scholarships, career opportunities, and self-learning resources. We provide guidance on applications, CV writing, personal statements, and more.

Is MBScholarships free to use?

Yes! MBScholarships is completely free to use. Our goal is to make education accessible by providing scholarship information and career resources at no cost.

Do you offer scholarships directly?

No, we do not provide scholarships directly. We connect students with verified scholarship opportunities from universities, governments, and organizations worldwide.

How can I find scholarships that match my profile?

You can browse scholarships on our website by filtering based on your study level, field of study, and country of interest. We also provide scholarship application tips to increase your chances of success.

Can I get help with my scholarship application?

Yes! We offer guides on writing personal statements, CVs, and recommendation letters. You can also find tips on preparing for scholarship interviews.

Can I contact MBScholarhips for support?

Absolutely! You can reach us via email, live chat, or social media for any inquiries related to scholarships, career guidance, or website support.

Who we are

Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between students and life-changing opportunities, ensuring accessibility, guidance, and success.
